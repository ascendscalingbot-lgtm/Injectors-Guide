Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this course for?
This is for RNs, NPs, PAs, and MDs/DOs who want to break into aesthetics. You must have an active medical license to eventually inject, but you can take the course to learn regardless of your current status.
Do I get a certificate?
Yes. Upon completion of all modules and assessments, you receive a Certificate of Completion in Aesthetic Injectables signed by Traci Andreason, FNP-C. This is crucial for your portfolio when applying for jobs.
Can I really learn this online?
Absolutely. In fact, many students find it better than in-person because you can pause, rewind, and re-watch complex anatomy and injection angles. In a 2-day live course, if you blink, you miss it. Here, you master the material completely.
How long do I have access?
Forever. You get Lifetime Access. This includes any updates we make to the course in the future as industry trends change.
Is there a discount if I sign up with a friend?
Yes! We love when colleagues learn together. If you and a friend enroll at the same time, we offer a special partner discount. Please reach out to our support team before purchasing to receive your unique coupon code.
Is hands-on training available?
Yes. Completing this online course makes you eligible for exclusive hands-on training days with Traci Andreason. Once you finish the curriculum and pass your assessments, you unlock the ability to book in-person clinical training to refine your technique.
Is hands-on training included in the Beginner Injectables Course?
Hands-on is not included in the course itself — and that's by design. The Beginner Injectables Course is built to give you a strong didactic foundation first: the anatomy, theory, clinical reasoning, and safety principles that make hands-on training actually stick. Without that foundation, hands-on time can feel overwhelming and the learning doesn't transfer as well. Think of this as building your confidence before you ever pick up a syringe.

Completing the course does make you eligible to train hands-on with me personally here in Utah — a 1:1 experience customized entirely around where you are and what you need.
Can I do hands-on training with you after the course?
Yes. Completing the Beginner Injectables Course makes you eligible for private hands-on training with me. It's completely custom — we work at your pace, on your goals — and is billed at $800/hour. Because you'll arrive with a real knowledge base from the course, we can use that time efficiently and go much deeper than a generic weekend course would allow.
Is this course worth it if I can't travel to Utah for hands-on?
Absolutely. Many students are based outside of Utah and complete the course for the didactic education alone — and find it genuinely transformative. For $1,500, you're getting structured, expert-led training that most weekend courses don't come close to covering at that depth, and at a fraction of the cost ($4–6k is common for a weekend format). Whether or not you ever join me in Utah, you'll walk away with real, applicable knowledge and the confidence to keep growing.
How is this different from a weekend hands-on course?
Weekend courses pack a lot into a short time — which can be exciting, but it's also a lot to absorb before you've built a foundation. The Beginner Injectables Course lets you learn at your own pace, revisit material as many times as you need, and genuinely understand the why behind what you're doing. If and when you're ready for hands-on, that time becomes far more valuable because you're not starting from zero. You're refining, not just surviving the weekend.
Why should I pay for an online course when I could just do a hands-on course instead?
A weekend hands-on course is a great experience — but it runs $4,000–$6,000, it's over in 48 hours, and you're often learning alongside a group with limited 1:1 attention. The Beginner Injectables Course gives you a thorough, self-paced education at a much lower entry point. And if you choose to add hands-on training with me afterward, you'll get custom time with me — not a group setting — and you'll be ready to make the most of every hour.
