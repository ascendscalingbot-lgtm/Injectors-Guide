Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this course for?
This is for RNs, NPs, PAs, and MDs/DOs who want to break into aesthetics. You must have an active medical license to eventually inject, but you can take the course to learn regardless of your current status.
Do I get a certificate?
Yes. Upon completion of all modules and assessments, you receive a Certificate of Completion in Aesthetic Injectables signed by Traci Andreason, FNP-C. This is crucial for your portfolio when applying for jobs.
Can I really learn this online?
Absolutely. In fact, many students find it better than in-person because you can pause, rewind, and re-watch complex anatomy and injection angles. In a 2-day live course, if you blink, you miss it. Here, you master the material completely.
How long do I have access?
Forever. You get Lifetime Access. This includes any updates we make to the course in the future as industry trends change.
Is there a discount if I sign up with a friend?
Yes! We love when colleagues learn together. If you and a friend enroll at the same time, we offer a special partner discount. Please reach out to our support team before purchasing to receive your unique coupon code.
Is hands-on training available?
Yes. Completing this online course makes you eligible for exclusive hands-on training days with Traci Andreason. Once you finish the curriculum and pass your assessments, you unlock the ability to book in-person clinical training to refine your technique.
