# COS-IG Automation Suggestions

These are the highest-leverage recurring automations to build first.

## Daily

1. Blockers audit
   - Schedule: every morning.
   - Output: update `operations/blockers.md` with current blockers, owners, priority, and the exact ask for Traci.
   - Why it matters: keeps "What are your blockers?" always answerable.

2. Feature upgrade suggestions
   - Schedule: daily.
   - Output: add 3-5 dashboard/product improvements to the Feature Upgrade Suggestions queue with impact, effort, and approve/deny status.
   - Why it matters: compounds the COS-IG ecosystem and keeps the dashboard improving.

3. Daily growth brief
   - Schedule: every morning.
   - Output: revenue/spend/enrollment summary, open decisions, alerts, and priorities.
   - Why it matters: gives Traci a founder-ready operating snapshot.

4. Alerts scan
   - Schedule: every 1-3 hours after credentials are connected.
   - Output: flag ROAS drops, spend anomalies, ad disapprovals, checkout issues, and stale approvals.
   - Why it matters: protects the 5x ROAS floor.

5. Content queue generator
   - Schedule: daily.
   - Output: one blog brief or draft, three short-form scripts, five ad concepts, and a keyword log.
   - Why it matters: keeps compounding channels moving.

## Weekly

6. Monday performance report
   - Schedule: Monday morning.
   - Output: KPI report using the master template, reconciled against Stripe where possible.
   - Why it matters: turns activity into executive learning.

7. Friday compliance and brand audit
   - Schedule: Friday afternoon.
   - Output: review published content, ads, and pitches for claims, tone, protected references, and missing approvals.
   - Why it matters: protects the brand.

8. Competitor and feature scan
   - Schedule: weekly until monthly cadence is enough.
   - Output: competitor pricing/content/ad changes plus feature upgrades for the dashboard.
   - Why it matters: keeps COS-IG strategically aware.

## Monthly

9. Monthly retro and budget proposal
   - Schedule: first day of each month.
   - Output: channel mix, ROAS, CPE, learnings, next-month budget recommendation, and strategic priorities.
   - Why it matters: keeps growth intentional rather than reactive.
